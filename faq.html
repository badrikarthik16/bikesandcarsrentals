Frequently Asked Question's

Q Who can hire a bike/car ?

All you need is a driving licence to hire a bike/car from us. We rent out to anyone with a driving licence and a passion for riding. However we reserve the right to refuse to rent our bike without assigning any reason. Be assured this is done only in extreme cases and so, most bikers can easily take a bike/car from us without any hassle

Q “I lost my driving licence” or “I do not have a driving licence” or “I have applied for a driving licence” or “I have a temporary driving licence” or “my driving licence is not valid in India”. Can I Hire a bike/car from you?

Without a valid normal driving licence that allows you to drive a two wheeler with gear, we wont let you drive our vehicle. And there is no exception

Q Do you help with our tour planning?

Our expert advisors are available for detailed consultation and assistance regarding your tour. Once you have made the booking, our consultation regarding tour is complimentary.

Q What other documents do I need to carry?

You must submit original Driving license and one of the following documents at the time of taking delivery of the bike. Both documents will be photocopied and returned to you immediately. The second document can be either Aadhar Card, Voter ID card or Passport

Q When and how do I pay the rental amount?

50% of the hiring charge must be paid in advance at the time of confirming the booking. The balance amount for hiring the bike as well as the Security deposit is to be paid before taking the bike key.

Q Apart from the bike what else would you supply?

We will provide you with a helmet for the rider and if you have a pillion, we will provide you with two helmets. You can also bring your own Helmets. We also provide you with a basic maintenance kit which will help you in case of an emergency. We will also provide you with all the necessary documents that you will need to carry regarding the bike.
